Tiger

The stripes that a tiger has are as unique as human fingerprints.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Tigris
  • Avarage Lenght: 3,2 meters (Male)
  • Avarage Lifespan: 23 years
  • Avarage Speed: 57 kmph
  • Habitat: Tropical rainforests, savannah, grasslands, rocky mountains

The tiger (Panthera tigris) is the largest cat species, most recognizable for its pattern of dark vertical stripes on reddish-orange fur with a lighter underside.
